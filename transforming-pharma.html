The client, a leading pharmaceutical company, faced challenges in financial reporting and cost management. They sought software solutions to improve efficiency.

What We Did?

In the current fast-paced business landscape, custom inventory management and automated business intelligence reporting are crucial for success. At DataVision, we focus on delivering personalized solutions that enhance data analytics, provide cost insight analysis, and optimize workflows to help organizations meet their operational objectives more efficiently.

Custom Inventory Management Software

Challenge: The client required a solution to enhance visibility and management of inventory across multiple plants and geographic regions.

Solution: Inventory Visibility: Comprehensive insights into stock levels, including excess, expiring, and expired products across various locations.

Excess Stock and Expiration Management: Detailed reports on excess and expiring inventory, coupled with actionable plans for usage or disposal.

Open Purchase Order Recommendations: Suggestions for managing open purchase orders, optimizing cash flow, and enhancing inventory turnover.

Automation of the Business Intelligence Reporting System

Challenge: The client required an efficient way to manage real-time data for C-Suite and Sales Executives. The absence of a comprehensive reporting system hindered quick decision-making in a fast-paced market.

Solution: We developed an advanced Business Intelligence Reporting system featuring: Live Sales/Margin Reports: Providing real-time updates on sales performance and profit margins to facilitate informed business decisions.

Live Sales/Margin Reports: Providing real-time updates on sales performance and profit margins to facilitate informed business decisions.

Gross to Net Calculation for the US Market: Accurate revenue calculations after accounting for discounts, returns, and allowances.

Budget vs. Actuals: A straightforward comparison of budgeted figures against actual performance to identify variances and support strategic planning.

Cost Insight Analyzer

Challenge: The company needed a systematic approach to analyse product costs effectively.

Solution: We implemented a robust cost insight analyzer featuring:

BOM-based Cost Sheets: Enabling detailed breakdowns of costs associated with each product, allowing for precise cost management.

What-If Analysis: A modelling feature that enabled stakeholders to evaluate the impact of raw material price changes on the overall Profit and Loss (P&L) statement.

Key Outcomes.

Increased Financial Efficiencies: Enhanced cost containment measures resulted in significantly improved overall financial performance.

Inventory Reduction: Achieved a reduction of 30-40% in inventory levels, allowing for substantial decreases in investment tied up in stock

Real-Time Reporting: Instant availability of reports empowered the management team with timely insights for strategic decision-making.

Conclusion.

Through the implementation of the Business Intelligence Reporting System, the Cost Analysis Tool, and Custom Inventory Management Software, the client was able to significantly enhance their operational efficiency. These innovative solutions yielded improved financial health and streamlined inventory management practices, positioning the company for sustained success in the competitive pharmaceutical industry.
